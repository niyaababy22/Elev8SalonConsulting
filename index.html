About Elev8 Salon Consulting

At Elev8 Salon Consulting, we provide advanced education in cosmetology and barbering that goes beyond standard beauty school training. Built on decades of real-world experience, our programs empower newly licensed professionals and experienced stylists with practical techniques they can immediately apply in their salon or barbershop.

ELEV8 Training empowers beauty professionals to elevate their careers through 8 industry-driven modules focused on guest experience, financial growth, leadership development, and salon success.

Our Experience / Mission

The mission of ELEV8 Training is to elevate beauty professionals by bridging the gap between technical education and real-world salon success through 8 business modules focused on leadership development, and income-building strategies.
